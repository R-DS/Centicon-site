CENTICON PTY LTD is a registered and insured construction company incorporated in Melbourne, Australia. We provide quality tiling, paving, waterproofing, screeding, caulking flooring, and painting services in Melbourne and in surrounding cities within Victoria.

At CENTICON, we take pride in the quality of our craftsmanship that is driven by a team of highly skilled, experienced, and dedicated workforce. Our people are the driving force behind our mission to provide quality construction services that bring value to clients and the Australian construction industry.

Waterproofing

It is essential to prevent water damages in a building to avoid costly repairs, protect the indoor valuables, and safeguard the wellbeing of the occupants.
