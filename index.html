Our Services Offered

Tiling

We specialize in providing residential and commercial floor and wall tiling services in Melbourne, Australia. Our team has expertise in using various tiling materials, ranging from any nature stone works to porcelain, and ceramic tiles. So whether you intend to tile the building interior or the outdoor environment, our team of experienced tilers will handle all the tasks from first to last

Screeding

Our team of highly experienced professionals has a track record of screeding projects to achieve a building's floor objectives. As construction professionals, we understand the procedure required to applying screeding products to achieve various goals. Whether you intend to leverage screeding technology to control sound/heat or enhance structural strength/aesthetic finishing to floor, we are up to the task.

Waterproofing

It is essential to prevent water damages in a building to avoid costly repairs, protect the indoor valuables, and safeguard the wellbeing of the occupants. Therefore, we provide comprehensive waterproofing services designed to keep your building safe from different types of water damages, from roof leakage to basement seepage and everything in-between.

Caulking

We provide comprehensive caulking services for building locations besides the common areas like the kitchen and bathroom, toilets, basin, sinks, and showers. In addition, our team has the technical expertise to handle caulking for doors and window components, tiles, plaster, precast, and brickworks. These are locations often overlooked by most artisans.

Painting

The painting of buildings is one of the areas of our specialization. Our team handles buildings' interior and exterior painting to give an excellent curb appeal and attractive aesthetics for the building interiors. We handle painting for residential, commercial, and industrial complexes.
